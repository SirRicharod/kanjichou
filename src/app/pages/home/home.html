What is Kanjichou?

Kanjichou is a combination of the Japanese words:

  • kanji (漢字): The Chinese characters used in Japanese writing.
  • techou (手帳): A notebook.

It's a place to keep track of the kanji you've learned and the ones you're still learning.

kanjichou uses kanjiapi.dev which provides data for over 13,000 kanji!
